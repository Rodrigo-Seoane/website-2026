## Part 1: UX Research Findings — State of the Art for AI-Powered Funnels (2026)

### 1.1 Key Market Shifts

The marketing funnel landscape has fundamentally changed in 2025–2026. AI-powered search features have reduced organic traffic significantly across categories, and nearly 800 million people weekly now use ChatGPT to compare options and plan next steps. This means your funnel must be optimized not just for Google, but for AI discoverability — being the clear, credible answer when AI tools recommend solutions.

For a solopreneur service business like yours, the winning approach is not to compete on volume or spend. It's to compete on **signal clarity** — making your offer so specific and well-structured that both human visitors and AI systems can immediately understand what you do, who you help, and why you're the right choice.

### 1.2 Funnel Architecture: What Works in 2026

The most effective low-budget funnel for a B2B design service follows this pattern:

**Traffic Source → Lead Magnet Landing Page → Email Nurture Sequence → Discovery Call Booking**

This is not new, but what's changed is that AI tools can now handle 70–80% of the execution across each stage, dramatically reducing your time investment.

### 1.4 Lead Magnet Recommendations

Based on your Brand Story Script and target audience (SaaS/App PMs struggling with retention and onboarding), the highest-converting lead magnet formats for your niche are:

**Option A — "UX Health Check" Self-Assessment (Recommended)**  
A quick interactive scorecard (5–8 questions) that SaaS PMs can take to diagnose friction points in their onboarding/retention. They get an instant score + personalized recommendations. You get their email + insight into their pain points.

**Option B — "Onboarding Teardown" PDF**  
A 5–7 page PDF showing a before/after teardown of a real onboarding flow, with your expert commentary. Positions you as the authority immediately.

**Option C — "Retention Killer Checklist"**  
A simple 1-page checklist of the 12 most common UX mistakes that drive churn in SaaS apps. Quick to consume, highly shareable.

**Recommendation:** Start with Option C (fastest to create, lowest friction to download) and evolve toward Option A as you validate the funnel.

### 2.3 Google Ads Strategy (€150/month)

With €150/month (~€5/day), you need to be surgical. Here's the approach:

**Campaign 1: High-Intent Search (€120/month)**  
Target keywords where people are actively looking for help:
- "UX audit for SaaS" 
- "improve app onboarding"
- "reduce SaaS churn UX"
- "user retention consultant"
- "product designer for SaaS"

At an estimated CPC of €2–4 for these niche B2B terms, you'd get roughly 30–60 clicks/month. With a 5% conversion rate to lead magnet download, that's 2–3 qualified leads/month from search alone.

**Campaign 2: Lead Magnet Downloads (€30/month)**  
Broader, cheaper keywords driving to the free checklist:
- "onboarding best practices SaaS"
- "how to reduce churn rate"
- "UX checklist mobile app"

**Important:** Send search ads directly to the Systeme.io landing page (not your homepage). Single-purpose pages convert 2–5x better.

### 2.4 Meta Ads Strategy (€50/month)

Don't try to compete for cold traffic on Meta with €50. Instead, use it exclusively for **retargeting**:

Install the Meta Pixel on rodrigoseoane.com. Then run a single retargeting campaign showing a testimonial-style ad or a "grab the free checklist" offer to people who visited your site but didn't take action. Retargeting audiences are small but warm, so €50/month is enough to stay visible.

### 2.5 LinkedIn Organic Strategy (€0)

You already have 8 LinkedIn articles. The play here is repurposing, not creating from scratch:

- **Monday:** Share a key insight from one of your existing articles (rewritten as a short LinkedIn post)
- **Thursday:** Share a quick UX tip, mini case study, or "before/after" screenshot

This is ~20 min/week using Claude to help adapt your existing content into LinkedIn-native posts.

### 2.6 Email Nurture Sequence (One-Time Setup)

Write these 5 emails once, then they run on autopilot via Systeme.io:

**Email 1 (Day 0) — "Here's your Retention Killer Checklist"**  
Deliver the PDF. Brief intro about who you are. Set expectations for what's coming.

**Email 2 (Day 2) — "The #1 onboarding mistake I see in SaaS apps"**  
Pure value. Share a specific, actionable insight from your 25+ years. No selling.

**Email 3 (Day 5) — "How [Client Type] reduced churn by fixing one flow"**  
A mini case study (anonymized if needed). Show your process and results.

**Email 4 (Day 8) — "Is your UX quietly costing you users?"**  
Address the internal problem from your Brand Story: the frustration of not knowing what's wrong, the pressure to ship without research, the high acquisition costs driven by poor retention.

**Email 5 (Day 12) — "Want me to take a look?"**  
Direct CTA to book a Discovery Call. Mention the 3 plan tiers briefly. Link to Calendly.

## Part 3: Why This Approach (Not Others)

**Why not a complex multi-channel AI automation stack?**  
Because you have 5 hours/week max. Complex setups with 5+ tools connected via Zapier require ongoing maintenance, debugging, and learning curves. A simpler system you actually run consistently beats a sophisticated one you abandon in week 3.

**Why Google Ads over LinkedIn Ads?**  
LinkedIn Ads have minimum CPCs of €5–8 for B2B targeting, which would burn through €200 in under a week with minimal data. Google Search captures people actively looking for solutions — higher intent at lower cost for niche B2B services.

**Why Systeme.io over building it into your Next.js site?**  
Your portfolio site (rodrigoseoane.com) should remain focused on showcasing work and authority. The funnel landing page is a different beast — it needs to be laser-focused on a single conversion action with no navigation distractions. Systeme.io gives you A/B testing, email automation, and analytics out of the box without adding complexity to your codebase.

**Why not invest in a fancy AI chatbot on your site?**  
Most AI chatbot tools for lead gen (Qualified, Drift, Intercom) start at $50–500/month and require significant setup. Your traffic volume doesn't justify this yet. Revisit when you're getting 500+ monthly visitors to your services pages.
